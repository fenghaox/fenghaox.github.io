About Me

Currently, I am a research associate professor at School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Southeast University. Before join SEU, I have obtained my PhD degree at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2023. During my PhD period (Oct. 2019 - Feb. 2020), I visited University of California, Irvine as a research scholar.

Research

My primary research interest lies in network and system security, with a special focus on mobile system security and IoT (Internet of Things) security.
